My Music Taste

I like a wide variety of music, but especially country music. I feel like the chill, acoustic, and (sometimes) wholesome vibe. I think some of the ideals protrayed in country music, including family, having fun, and the simple things in life. I also enjoy the twang of the music.

Although I still listen to much more music, my favorite kind has to be Country. There are many artists that make country music, often blending in other genres, including the following:

  • Pop Country
  • Rock Country
  • Hip-hop Country
  • Folk Country
  • Many more!
I enjoy most of these sub-genres, although some music hybrids are... interesting. Below are some of my favorite artists, some of whom participate in this blend of genres within Country Music.
